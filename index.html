An Online Library of All my Attempts to be Funny

Hello! My name is Harry Minsky, I'm a freshman at NYU studying at the college of arts and sciences, and as a side-hobby I enjoy all sorts of comedic content creation. In highschool I was the "funny friend," which meant I got a lotta laughs at the lunch table, and am incapable of sincerity in any form, even when my mind and brain and soul are in exestential agony! I spent some time doing comedy skits inside of school and out, and as such have a small collection of scripts and videos that I've made. Additionally, I've been trying to write comedic prose this semester to varying degrees of success. Enjoy(or detest) flipping through the various things I've created, and hopefully I'll continue to update this page if the New York comedy scene doesn't crush my spirit like a grape.
