Scripts

These are three of my favorite scripts I wrote for my highschool sketch troupe. There are several major differences between writing prose and writting for performing. Often sketches that read very well perform poorly, the opposite is less common but it still happens! I'm not a great authority on the subject but my sense is that sketches that read well focus too heavily on dialogue and wind up being more of a monolgoue/dialogue then a sketch. Good dialogue is essential in a sketch, but the audience likes to see movement! Activity! Sensless Debauchery! You gotta give the people what they want.
